Overview

Purpose

My name is Enmanuel De los Santos, I’ve been working with computers with for some time till now, and you’ll be impressed of how many problems we can solve with it. This is why we created PetServices. Never wondered why your dog got sick when you gave him chocolate? Or why your cat stopped being energetic as he used to be? A lot of animals suffer in silence or do not get the attention they deserve because we do not know how to identify when they need it. At PetServices we are compromised to assist you so you can give your pet the best treatment they deserve. Our mission is that you and you cute friends can have an insanely great experience together.

Audience

This website is designed for everyone who wants to know how to take care of their animals, or for those who already know who do it and want to learn more. All our information here well organized and easy to read so everyone from every age range can read it. Starting with kids who have his or her first pet, teenagers who are starting in this world, or people who already have their pets and want to give them better treatment.
