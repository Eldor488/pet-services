Purpose

My name is Enmanuel De los Santos, I’ve been working with computers for some time till now, and you’ll be impressed with how many problems we can solve them. This is why I created PetZone, For those looking to buy or adopt a dog, cat, horse, rabbit, fish, bird, or reptile, you are in the right place. Audience

This website is designed for everyone who wants to know how to take care of their animals, or for those who already know who do it and want to learn more. All our information here well organized and easy to read so everyone from every age range can read it. Starting with kids who have his or her first pet, teenagers who are starting in this world, or people who already have their pets and want to give them better treatment.

Content

Home Page

What kind of audience will be on my site?

In my web site plan one of the things I stated was that my I webpage would be directed to all kind of audiences, since people who are having their first pet, or for those who have a lot of experience and just got here out of curiosity. I will make the information pretty easy-to-read in my website, thinking in those people who have not experience with their pets or maybe do not the name of the things they have to use to give their pets a better life. What shampoo does my dog need to use, what kind of food I need to give to my birds? Why my cat does not like that toy I bought her? Just to name a few. In the statement of my website, I said that I will answer questions for all kind of people, from beginners to the most experienced ones.

Images for the Home page

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

All the questions I will answer in the website, will come from all kind of topics regarding your pets. Their health, that for me is one of the more important ones. What kind of food can I give to my dog? How can I keep my cat clean? Which vaccines do my dog need? Which sport activities can I practice with my hamster? But I will not just talk about health here, that will be a little too boring for my visitors. Something really important that I believe everyone should know, it is the emotional part, and the emotional connections that we make with our little friends. How can I know if my dog is enjoying his time with me? Why my cat stopped being happy as usual? What can I do to give a more active life to my dog? The hygiene is something that some people ignore, and this can have I huge impact on your pet’s life, having a good hygiene with your pets can prevent a lot of disease and unnecessary risks in the future. Something a lot of people wonder when they go about this matter of hygiene, is that they do not know how to give a good one to their pets. Some of us do not know how to give a good shower to our dog, or we wonder if we even have to shower them. Some question we answer in the page would be like: “Which kind of shampoo do I need to buy for my dog? How many times per week do I need to shower my dog, how often do I need to change the water of my fishes?”. Our goal here is that we provide all the important information, so our customer can have an amazing great experience visiting our web pages.

Images for the Page 2

Why people will visit my site?

My site will call people attention because of its simplicity. It will not have any complicated vocabulary, or some unnecessary steps that people need to go through in some other websites. Our goal is that as soon as you get in our site you can get your information in the fastest way possible, and exactly what you need. Nothing about unnecessary details, just what you need, and no more than that.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

One of our related pages will have a form, where, you will put your inquiries and get more information from us. You will receive this information via e-mail. In this form you will be asked to put your name, e-mail address and your inquiry. And we will get in touch with you as soon as possible.
